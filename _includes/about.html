About SCR

The Southern California Robotics Symposium (SCR) is a two-day technical symposium that aims to bring together roboticists from around Southern California. With a high density of academic institutions, research centers, and industry players, SCR is expected to have a large audience with broad expertise and interests. In particular, SCR encourages early-stage academics to present their work to the local, supportive robotics community. Additionally, this symposium provides an opportunity for established and up-and-coming companies to showcase their products and recruit young talent.

Finally after a two-year hiatus, we are excited to bring SCR back in September 2022 at UCLA, with a focus on in-person interactions. Thus, the highlight of our symposium will be the poster session, which we hope will foster more research discussions and networking opportunities than traditional conference talks. A major strength of SCR that sets us apart is that our conference is small and local, which allows for lasting engagement, partnerships, and collaborations beyond the event.
