How to Contribute

  1. Posters: The main event of this symposium; everyone is invited to apply for a poster. An excellent opportunity to initiate discussions, network, and establish long-lasting collaborations with fellow colleagues across a diverse range of robotic disciplines.
  2. Workshops: Hands-on demonstrations and tutorials designed to fully engage the audience such as prototyping, testing new products, etc.
  3. New Faculty and Rising Stars Speakers: Early-stage academics (0-5 years), postdocs, and late-stage doctoral students are invited to apply for a talk with the focus being the human impact of their work.
