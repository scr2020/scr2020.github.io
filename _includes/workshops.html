Workshops

We are now accepting workshop/tutorial proposals for {{site.conference_title_short}} {{site.year}}. We will select a limited number of hands-on, tutorial-style workshops to be conducted. If you are interested in holding a workshop at {{site.conference_title_short}} {{site.year}}, please submit the following:

  • A three-page proposal describing the activities of the proposed workshop including the following:
    • IEEE style, PDF format, size 10pt font
    • Title
    • List of organizers with contact information and indicate the primary contact for the workshop
    • Abstract (200 words maximum) summarizing the proposed event
    • Detailed description of the event. Specifically highlight the interactive elements and the takeaways that participants can only learn through the hands-on experience of the workshop
    • Include a dedicated "Impacts" section addressing the real-world impacts and relevance of the workshop. How can the information and skills learned during this workshop be used to solve real-world problems?
    • Expected duration and number of participants
    • Required equipment and logistics
  • Submission deadline: July 31th, 2022, Midnight PDT
  • Accepted submissions will be announced on Aug. 15th, 2022, Midnight PDT

Please send your PDF submissions via e-mail to {{site.organizer-email}} with the subject "Workshop Proposal".
